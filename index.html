Overview

Problem and solution

INTRODUCTION

  • Pollution is the introduction of contaminants into the natural environment that cause adverse change.  
  • Pollution can take the form of chemical substances or energy, such as noise, heat, or light.  
  • Pollutants, the components of pollution, can be either foreign substances/energies or naturally occurring contaminants.  

OBJECTIVE

The main objective of the pollution monitoring dashboard is to provide the provide the details pollution that is happening in and across different state of the country. This dashboard is also aimed at creating an awareness to the public about the harmful effects of the pollution to the environment. This dashboard also has features to complain about the pollution issues that is happening in their neighborhood, so that appropriate actions can be taken to eliminate the pollution.
